Good questions

The things people ask first.

Does working with you cost me anything?

No. My help is free to you. I'm compensated by the insurance company when you enroll, and using a broker doesn't raise your premium. You'd pay the same rate going direct, just without the help.

Are you part of the government or healthcare.gov?

No. Carson Health Advising is a private, independent brokerage and isn't affiliated with or endorsed by any government agency. I can help you enroll in ACA Marketplace plans as a licensed broker, alongside private options.

What's the difference between ACA and private plans?

ACA Marketplace plans are the ones eligible for government subsidies based on income, and they cover essential health benefits. Private plans can offer different price points and structures that sometimes fit better depending on your situation. I'll walk you through how each one would work for you so the difference is clear before you decide.

Which areas do you cover?

I'm based in Florida and licensed in two dozen states. If you're outside Florida, reach out anyway, there's a good chance I can help or point you in the right direction.

Will I get blown up with calls and spam?

No. Your information comes to me directly, not a lead-selling network. I'll usually start with a text, and we go at your pace. You can opt out anytime.
